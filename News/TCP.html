T.I.P. now accepting applicants for second online TCP



Following the success of its first online Teaching Certificate Program (TCP), the Technological Institute of the Philippines (T.I.P.) Quezon City College of Education shall again be offering its pioneering post-baccalaureate program with focus on online teaching and learning.

Enrollment for this year's second online TCP, designed to help aspiring and experienced teachers cope in the new normal, is ongoing.

Classes are set thrice a week after office hours and will run from September 27 to December 22, 2021.

Topics covered in the 18-unit coursework include curriculum development, student learning assessment, and online teaching tools and strategies, among others.

Graduates of any field, and even practicing instructors who wish to enhance their remote teaching skills and/or qualify for the Board Licensure Examination for Professional Teachers (BLEPT), are welcome to enroll.

A Php 3,400 discount awaits those who will pay in full on or before September 27, 2021.

Slots are only limited. Enroll online via https://bit.ly/TIPTCPAdmission.

For more information on the Teaching Certificate Program, visit https://www.tip.edu.ph/coe_qc_campus.html or T.I.P.’s official social media accounts through @TIP1962official for Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. For enrollment inquiries, contact tcp@tip.edu.ph or (+63) 917-859-0279.
